## Copy Rules

### Absolute Rules

- **No trademark symbols** (™, ®) anywhere except the tagline itself
- **No emojis** anywhere on the site
- **No exclamation points** in clinical or professional copy
- **"Physician"** not "provider" in brand-facing contexts
- **"Health before healthcare™"** — always the full phrase, never shortened

### Audience-Specific Copy

**Hero / Journey sections:**
> Built by anesthesiologists and surgeons

**About section (full audience acknowledgment):**
> Anesthesiologists, surgeons, intensivists, and health systems leaders

**Hero section label:**
> Evidence-based surgical preparation

**Hero headline:**
> The evidence-based prep tool for patients and their physicians.

**Hero subheadline:**
> Personalized, guideline-driven recommendations for every patient — whether you're preparing for surgery yourself or optimizing a patient you care for.

**Journey section title:**
> One tool. Two perspectives.

**For physicians section title:**
> Evidence-based perioperative decision support, built for time-pressured clinicians.

### Tone by Audience

| Audience | Tone |
|---|---|
| Patient | Warm, clear, action-oriented. No jargon. Priority labels: Important / Recommended / Optional |
| Physician | Precise, efficient, scannable. Clinical terminology expected and preferred. |
| Health system (secondary) | Outcomes-focused. Reference ERAS integration, risk reduction. Keep secondary to patient/physician framing. |

## Hero Section Layout

Split two-card layout:
- **Left card:** "I'm preparing for surgery" → links to Pre-Op Assessment (patient role)
- **Right card:** "I'm a physician or care team" → links to Pre-Op Assessment (provider role)

Health systems are referenced only as a subtle secondary text link below the cards ("Are you a hospital or health system? Learn about our clinical partnerships").
